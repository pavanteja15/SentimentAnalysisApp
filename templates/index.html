What is Sentiment Analysis?

Sentiment Analysis is a method of determining the tone or emotion behind a piece of text. It can categorize text as Positive, Neutral, or Negative based on the sentiment.

Sample Sentences:

  • "I am so happy with the new update, it's fantastic!"
  • "This is the worst experience I've ever had. Truly disappointing."
  • "I am not going to the grocery store today"
  • "Absolutely love this! Highly recommended."
  • "Neutral on this one, Its ok"
  • I am not going to the grocery store today, yesterday I had a terrible experience
